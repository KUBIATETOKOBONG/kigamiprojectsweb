About Kigami

Kigami Projects is a community-focused initiative that makes it easier to shop and support sustainably. We curate and share local, preloved goods every Thursday, allowing members to shop via WhatsApp with ease.

Our goal is to foster a connected, resourceful community through donations, volunteering, and conscious consumption. We believe in making sustainability accessible and local, using familiar tools like WhatsApp to reach more people with less waste.

But Kigami is more than just a shopping platform — it's a movement rooted in environmental protection, creativity, and community empowerment. As a registered Scottish Charitable Incorporated Organisation (SCIO), we are committed to rethinking waste, promoting circular practices, and turning everyday actions into long-term impact.

Whether you're donating, shopping, or volunteering, you're helping build a future where sustainability is not just a goal, but a shared way of life.
