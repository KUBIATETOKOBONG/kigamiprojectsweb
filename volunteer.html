Kigami Business Clean Support

Kigami Projects works with local businesses to help create cleaner, safer, and more environmentally responsible communities.

Our services include:

  • Litter picking and environmental maintenance
  • Bus stop cleaning and public space care
  • Waste awareness and sustainability support
  • Community engagement sessions
  • Staff clean-up activities

We work with:

  • Shops and cafés
  • Schools and offices
  • Community organisations

Businesses can request one-off clean-up support, awareness sessions, and community partnership activities.

Little Green Champions

A fun programme encouraging children and families to care for the environment through community clean-up activities.

Champions can:

  • Join family-friendly clean-ups
  • Help improve local spaces
  • Earn certificates and recognition
  • Become part of positive community change

The programme teaches teamwork, environmental responsibility, and community pride while making outdoor activities fun and rewarding.

Want your child to become a Little Green Champion? Join one of our upcoming community clean-up activities and become part of the movement for cleaner communities.
